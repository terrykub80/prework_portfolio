My Story

How I ended up here....

I've been in customer facing roles for almost 2 decades now and I can't remember a time I ever actually enjoyed it. Maybe I finally smartened up, or maybe it's a midlife crisis, either way I decided to completely change my trajectory. I quit selling insurance to people who don't want to buy insurance and started looking at options. A former employee reached out and told me about a coding bootcamp he had taken. After a few YouTube videos and some phone calls I decided that coding was something I'd be interested in learning more about. I have never had any experience with anything like this before but I've never shyed away from a challenge.

My Pets

Meet Sky, Mango, and Mikey....

I feel like I need to start by saying that I have a son. He's 20 and recently decided he doesn't want to live with his dad anymore, which is understandable. I say that to say this, I've always been an animal guy but it's gone to a whole new level since I became an empty nester. The three pictured above are now the recipients of countless hours of fatherly advice and terrible jokes.

Sky is the oldest. I've had her since 2013 and outside of my wife she's probably my best friend. She's old and set in her ways but she's a sweetheart.

Mikey is the smallest of the group but is without a doubt the one in charge. The truth is, Mikey doesn't give a crap about you, me, or anybody else. If he had money to buy food and thumbs to open it up he probably wouldn't still be living here. We're basically all here to make sure Mikey is happy.
